About Us

A perfect work of art of craftsmanship spread over 12.5 acres of lush gardens; it is a one-stop destination for entertainment, food & excitement. Chokhi Dhani Sonipat enlivens, a vivid Rajasthani legacy that abandons you awestruck with every one of the attractions and components of ethnic culture and convention. ATTRACTIONS

  • LIVE
  • FUN

BEEN PARTY (SNAKE CHARMERS)

Witness the musical ensemble of the snake charmers playing different instruments and entertaining the audience.

THE PUPPET SHOW - KATHPUTLI RO NAACH

The colourful Puppet Show transports you into the world of valiant Kings, beautiful princesses and all powerful magicians. The puppeteer makes episodes and scenes from their lives come alive to give you a glimpse of their lifestyle.

MEHENDI(HENNA)

Our experienced professional artists apply in intricate, beautiful patterns on your hands. And it is complimentary from Chokhi Dhani Sonipat.

THE MAGIC SHOW - JAADU RO KHEL

The magician performs his captivating show with some hand tricks and hypnotism which will puzzle your mind.

LIVE BAJRE KI ROTTI SERVED WITH GUR AND CHUTNEY

Enjoy the exotic taste of bajre ki roti with Gur (jaggery) and chutney cooked and prepared in front of you at Chokhi Dhani Sonipat

FOLK DANCE AND MUSIC SHOW - CHAUPAL NAACH GAAN RE

Witness different forms of Rajasthani folk dances such as Ghoomar, Potter Dance, Kalbeliya - the Snake Dance, Bhopa-Bhopi Dance, etc.

ASTROLOGISTS

Know your future by trained and learned astrologers only at Chokhi Dhani Sonipat

NAGADA PARTY

Let the beats of nagada and dhol shake your soul. Nagada party will surely make you groove over the beats.

FIRE PERFORMANCE

Get mesmerized and witness the skills that involve the manipulation of fire

CAMEL RIDE

Experience the excitement and fun of camel ride. Have a tour of entire Chokhi Dhani Sonipat in style, enjoying the sights and sounds at a comfortable pace.

BULLOCK CART RIDE

Explore the beautiful sights and the simple architecture of the entire village on a comfortable bullock cart.

TONGA RIDE

In the era of cars and bikes, tonga ride is a forgotten luxury. However, at Chokhi Dhani Sonipat we have all the arrangements made to make you travel back in time and experience the most amazing tonga ride.

SKILL GAMES

Enjoy trying your hand at the many skill games available like dart game & aiming, shooting games and much more. There are children play areas with slides and other junior activities to keep them entertained.

MINI TRAIN RIDE

Get ready to smile and recall those joyful and innocent memories of your childhood riding Mini Train only at Chokhi Dhani Sonipat.

BHOOL BHULAIYA

Do you love solving puzzles? Then bhool bhulaiya is for you which is a maze wherein you can enter from one gate and have to find your way to get out of from another gate.

GONG

That old school bell just got bigger in size. And you can ring it as many times as you want.

ETHNIC COSTUME PHOTOGRAPHY

In the era of selfies and Instagram why not get clicked wearing the ethnic rajasthani costumes and make the world go jealous.

ANCIENT CAVE - (GUFA JHARNA)

If you always wished to be an explorer then Gufa Jharna is for you. Feel the adventurous journey as you travel through this ancient cave.
