CORPORATES EVENTS

If your organization is in Delhi NCR, ChokhiDhaniSonipat is the perfect place for a corporate get- together. ChokhiDhaniSonipat is a charming village which provides a real rural backdrop of Rajasthani culture.


ChokhiDhaniSonipat is an ideal getaway for team building exercises, corporate occasions, product launches, merchant meets and gatherings, all in a conventional yet invigorating way.


Outfitted with all civilities for all corporate events, the fantastic dining facility and the decor which speaks about the tradition of Rajasthan. This village theme entertainment takes you on a voyage through Rajasthan's cultural hamlet in the days of abundance and glory.

E-mail: info@chokhidhanisonipat.comOur specialised solutions include:

  • Pure veg dinner-North Indian &Rajasthani Cuisine
  • Rides (any 4) : camel cart, bullock cart, tonga ride, tractor ride, columbus ride,
  • Skill games (any 4) : pyramid, airgun shooting, bow & arrow, dart board, basket the ball, bowling
  • Cultural folk dances : ghoomar, kalbeliya, terahtaali, bhopabhopi, been party, chaupal party
  • Puppet Show

SCHOOL PICNICS

GIVES US A RING: LET THE PICNIC BEGIN

ChokhiDhaniSonipat offers different activities that give immense pleasure and enjoyment to the kids. Apart from the general activities like tonga ride, camel rides, mehndi, and stoneware , there are some other exciting activities like dholki dance, magic shows, puppet shows, mini train rides, etc.


One distinct thing that school kids adore about ChokhiDhaniSonipat is the food. The authentic Rajasthani food that we offer is served on a leaf platter to give you the warm touch of Indian culture.


Apart from Rajasthani food we also serve North Indian Cuisine. Your taste buds will surely remember this treat for a long time. Come and experience true colors of exquisite Rajasthani heritage.
