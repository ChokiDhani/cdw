About Us

A perfect work of art of craftsmanship spread over 12.5 acres of lush gardens; it is a one-stop destination for entertainment, food & excitement. Chokhi Dhani Sonipat enlivens, a vivid Rajasthani legacy that abandons you awestruck with every one of the attractions and components of ethnic culture and convention.
