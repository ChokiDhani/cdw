About Us

A perfect work of art of craftsmanship spread over 12.5 acres of lush gardens; it is a one-stop destination for entertainment, food & excitement. Chokhi Dhani Sonipat enlivens, a vivid Rajasthani legacy that abandons you awestruck with every one of the attractions and components of ethnic culture and convention. Beginning from the welcome tilak that helps to recall "PADHARO MAHRE DES" neighbourliness of Rajasthan, the mandir, town bazaar, animal rides on horse, camel etc., bioscope, village style photograph studio, palm reading by learned jyotish pundits, artists applying mehendi, recreations zone, open gardens to unwind and bond with each otherâ€¦ , you'll savour each moment of this awesome destination of Chokhi Dhani Sonipat where time slows to a tropical tempo.
